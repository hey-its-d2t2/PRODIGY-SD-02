About the Game

Welcome to the Guess the Number Game! The objective is simple: guess the number that the computer has randomly selected. Each time you make a guess, you will be informed whether your guess was too high or too low. Keep guessing until you find the correct number.

Rules

  • Enter a number in the input field.
  • Click "Submit Guess" to see if you are correct.
  • If your guess is incorrect, you will be informed whether you need to guess higher or lower.
  • Continue guessing until you find the correct number.
  • Click "Start New Game" to begin a new round.
